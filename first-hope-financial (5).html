How It Works

Three honest steps. No fine print designed to trip you up.

Every First Hope account follows the same path — because the path itself is the point.

01

Open in about 10 minutes

Just a valid ID. No minimum deposit, no credit check. Apply from your phone or in person. Account number and virtual card ready immediately.

02

Save with structure, not luck

Every deposit round-ups into a goal you set yourself. We show you the line going up — because watching it matters as much as the number.

03

Borrow without the gauntlet

Fair-rate loans based on how you've actually used your account — not a score built before you ever walked in. Every on-time payment builds your history.

Account Tiers

Start free. Unlock more as you grow.

Every First Hope account opens at Tier 1 automatically — no cost, no catch. Upgrade at your own pace to access higher limits and more powerful tools.

Tier 1 — Foundation

A real account. Right now.

Assigned automatically when you open. No minimum deposit — start with $0, $50, $100, whatever you have.

Tier 1 restrictions
✗  No transfers or withdrawals
✗  No loans or community borrowing
✓  Deposit any amount, anytime — no minimum

Cash-style deposits simply grow your balance, like savings — they don't change your tier. Deposit via BTC or a linked gift card instead, and once those deposits add up to $200 or more — in one go or across several — you're upgraded to Tier 2 automatically. No application needed.

  • Full account number assigned instantly
  • Virtual debit card — issued immediately
  • Receive incoming transfers from others
  • Hope Savings goal visible (locked until Tier 2)
  • Spend tracking & SMS summaries
  • Zero overdraft fees, ever
Default — opens automatically on signup
Most popular
Tier 2 — Enhanced

Unlock transfers. Start borrowing.

Upgrade happens automatically — no application. Once your BTC or gift card deposits add up to $200 or more, you're moved to Tier 2 and the full amount lands in your balance.

  • Everything in Tier 1
  • Transfers & withdrawals unlocked
  • Community loan eligibility (from $200)
  • Transfer limit up to $10,000/day
  • International bank transfers enabled
  • Savings match up to $1,000/year
  • Priority in-app support
How the automatic upgrade works
Deposit Bitcoin (BTC) Build toward $200 in one deposit or several — upgrade is instant once you cross it
Link a gift card Gift card balance counts toward your $200 — stack it with BTC deposits if you like

Only BTC and gift card deposits count toward the $200. Cash deposits still add to your balance — they just don't trigger the upgrade.

Tier 3 — Premium

The full First Hope experience.

Order your physical FHF card and permanently unlock the highest tier — the card is shipped to your door.

  • Everything in Tier 2
  • Physical First Hope debit card (shipped to you)
  • Unlimited daily transfer limits
  • Exclusive member community access
  • NGO aid distribution eligibility
  • Dedicated account manager
